Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

What are the modes of payment accepted?

We accept PayNow for effective convenience.

What is Bridal Viewing?

A bridal viewing is a 1-hour complimentary consultation service to look at our gown collections and understand our packages.

Why is a 30% deposit required for the first payment?

Upon payment of a deposit, we confirm and secure your wedding date. This follows our first-come, first-served policy.

What if I change my package after making the deposit?

You can upgrade your package or add a-la-carte services, but downgrading is not allowed. Changes must be made before outfit selection.

How long can I rent the bridal outfits for?

The rental period is 3 days, including collection and return days. Collection is on Thursday or Friday, and returns on Monday (10 AM-12 PM).
