Let's get to know us more

Founder

Blending old-world charm with modern flair, founders of TheWeddingFavour are heritage-driven visionaries. With roots in traditional craft and savvy in contemporary design, we weave culture with style, creating bridal looks that honor the past and dazzle the present.

History

Founded with a vision to blend timeless traditions and modern elegance, TheWeddingFavour began as a passion project by local designers inspired by our cultural heritage and love for contemporary style. Starting with handcrafted pieces that paid homage to traditional craftsmanship, we quickly gained popularity for creating wedding attire that feels both classic and fresh. Over the years, TheWeddingFavour expanded, innovating to meet diverse bridal tastes while staying rooted in cultural authenticity.
